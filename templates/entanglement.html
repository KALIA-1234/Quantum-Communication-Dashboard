📘 About Quantum Entanglement

Quantum entanglement creates a strong relationship between two qubits. When one qubit is measured, the outcome of the second qubit is immediately correlated. In this simulation you should mainly observe the states |00⟩ and |11⟩, which demonstrates perfect quantum correlation. Entanglement is the foundation of quantum communication, quantum teleportation and quantum cryptography.
