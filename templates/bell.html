📘 About Bell States

Bell States demonstrate quantum entanglement, where two qubits share a correlated quantum state. The simulator produces approximately equal probabilities for |00⟩ and |11⟩, showing perfect correlation. These states are widely used in quantum communication, teleportation and quantum cryptography.
