📘 About Quantum Teleportation

Quantum teleportation is a protocol that transfers the quantum state of one qubit to another by combining:

✔ Quantum Entanglement
✔ Bell-State Measurement
✔ Classical Communication

The original quantum state is reconstructed on the destination qubit without physically moving the particle itself. This protocol is a fundamental building block for future quantum networks and the quantum internet.
